Another name for this era is "the Golden Age of Arcade Video Games."

This was a time when arcade video games entered pop culture and became a dominant cultural phenominom.

It lead to the rise of hits such as Pac-Man, Defender, Donkey Kong, and Centipede.

Besides arcades, this was also when the second generation of consoles came out, which included platforms such as: Fairchild, Channel F, Atari 2600, Intellivision, Odyssey, and ColecoVision.

This was marked the beginning of PC Gaming as home computers started appearing and hobbyist groups ported popular arcade games such as Space Invaders and Frogger.
